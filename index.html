I Love Drawing!

My Hobby Page — Made with ❤️ by Alex, Grade 5

👋 Hi, I'm Alex!

My name is Alex and I'm 10 years old. I'm in Grade 5 at Sunshine Elementary School. My favourite hobby is drawing! I love making art almost every single day. Whether it's doodling in my notebook or painting on a big canvas, art makes me really happy. I've been drawing since I was 4 years old — that's 6 whole years of practice! 🖍️

⭐ My 3 Favourite Things About Drawing

🌈

Playing with Colours

Mixing colours is like magic! Red + blue = purple. I love experimenting with watercolour paints to make cool colour combinations I've never seen before.

🦕

Drawing Animals & Dinosaurs

I especially love drawing dinosaurs and animals. My T-Rex drawings are my best ones! I use YouTube tutorials to learn how to make them look realistic.

📖

Making My Own Comic Books

I've made 5 mini comic books so far! My favourite one is about a superhero dog named Captain Biscuit who saves the world from an evil cat. 🐶🦸

💚 Why I Like Drawing

Drawing is special to me for so many reasons. Here's what I love the most:

😌 It helps me relax when I feel stressed or tired.
💡 It lets me share my big ideas and wild imagination!
🏆 I feel proud when I finish a drawing I worked hard on.
👫 My friends love when I draw pictures of them as superheroes!

✏️ Thanks for visiting my page! Keep creating and never stop drawing! 🎨✨
